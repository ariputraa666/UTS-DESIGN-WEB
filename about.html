ABOUT

Interior is a matter related to the creative field along with technical solutions that are applied to the building structure. Interior is also a term relating to the inside of a building. Meanwhile, according to Big Indonesian Dictionary (KBBI), the interior is intended for the inside of the building, both the room and so on or the arrangement of furniture, for example decoration in the building space, The side of the room which includes the interior is everything that is functional, to improve the quality of life and culture of its inhabitants, as well as to give an impression in terms of aesthetics or beauty. Among other things, related to space, tools, and humans as users.

ROOM

In the world of interior design, we have the opportunity to maximize the three dimensions of space, namely length, width and height. These three dimensions of space can be filled or left empty, depending on what you want to achieve in terms of functionality and design.

LINES

Horizontal, vertical and dynamic lines can help shape a room and "guide" our view. Creating lines using furniture and objects as well as structural design in a room can establish harmony, continuity and contrast Besides that, there are also dynamic lines that refer to diagonal, zigzag, or curved lines. These lines can be seen in several elements in the room, such as the stairs, which give energy and movement to the structure of the room.
